Repair

Words for mending connections and rebuilding trust.

  • "I was wrong."
  • "I hurt you."
  • "This is on me."
  • "I am truly sorry."
  • "I hear you."
  • "Your feelings are valid."
  • "I will do better."
  • "How can I make this right?"
  • "What do you need from me?"
  • "Take all the time you need."
  • "I will keep showing up."
  • "I will show you through my actions."
Repair takes courage. These words are the first step back to each other.
