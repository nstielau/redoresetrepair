Redo

When words come out wrong, just ask for a do-over.

  • "Can I have a redo?" simple, direct, works for everyone
  • "Let me try that again." takes ownership without over-explaining
  • "Rewind!" playful, breaks tension
  • "That came out wrong. What I meant was..." when the words didn't match the intention
  • "I didn't like how I said that." honest acknowledgment, models self-reflection
  • "Wait, pause. Let me start over." buys a moment to collect yourself
  • "That was my grumpy voice. Here's my kind voice..." names the problem concretely
  • "I want to say that in a nicer way." clear intention, easy for kids too
  • "Can we pretend I didn't say that?" lighter touch, good for minor slip-ups
  • "Hold on — I need a redo." asserts the need without apology
Anyone can ask for a redo, anytime. No shame. Just try again kinder.
