Rupture

Words that harm connection. Be aware and avoid.

  • "Uh"
  • "Duh"
  • "Whatever"
  • "Fine"
  • "Jesus Christ"
  • "Here we go again"
  • "You always..." / "You never..."
  • "I told you so"
  • "Calm down"
  • "Forget it"
  • "I don't care"
  • "Just drop it"
Notice these slipping out? That's a signal — time for a reset or a redo.
