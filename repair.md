# Repair

Words and phrases for relationship repair - mending connections and rebuilding trust.

## Acknowledging Harm

- "I was wrong"
- "I hurt you"
- "I made a mistake"
- "I let you down"
- "I failed to consider your feelings"
- "I wasn't there for you when you needed me"
- "I broke your trust"

## Taking Responsibility

- "I own this"
- "This is on me"
- "I am responsible for what happened"
- "There is no excuse for my behavior"
- "I should have known better"
- "I chose poorly"

## Expressing Genuine Remorse

- "I am truly sorry"
- "I deeply regret what I did"
- "I wish I could take it back"
- "I feel terrible about how this affected you"
- "Knowing I caused you pain is hard to sit with"

## Validating Their Experience

- "Your feelings are valid"
- "You have every right to be upset"
- "I understand why you feel this way"
- "I hear you"
- "Your reaction makes complete sense"
- "I would feel the same way"

## Committing to Change

- "I will do better"
- "Here is what I will do differently"
- "I am working on this"
- "I commit to changing this behavior"
- "You will see a difference"
- "I am taking steps to ensure this does not happen again"

## Asking for a Path Forward

- "What do you need from me?"
- "How can I make this right?"
- "What would help you feel safe again?"
- "I want to earn back your trust"
- "Can we talk about how to move forward?"
- "I am here when you are ready"

## Giving Space

- "Take all the time you need"
- "I will be here when you are ready"
- "I respect your need for distance"
- "There is no pressure"
- "Your timeline matters"

## Rebuilding Trust

- "I will show you through my actions"
- "Words are not enough - I will prove it"
- "Consistency is my commitment"
- "I will keep showing up"
- "Trust is rebuilt slowly, and I am patient"
