# Reset

Words and phrases for when you need to step away to collect yourself.

## Taking a Short Break

- "You know what? I'm a bit overwhelmed. I'm gonna go do a reset for 15 minutes."
- "I need to step away for a few minutes to clear my head."
- "Let me take a quick break so I can come back with fresh eyes."
- "I'm going to pause here and reset - back in 10."

## When Emotions Are Running High

- "I want to give this the attention it deserves. Let me take a moment."
- "I'm feeling a lot right now. Can we pick this back up in a bit?"
- "I need to decompress before we continue this conversation."
- "Let's take a breather and come back to this."

## In Meetings or Group Settings

- "I need a quick reset - can we take five?"
- "Would it be okay if we paused for a few minutes? I could use a moment."
- "I'm going to step out briefly to recharge."

## Professional Contexts

- "I want to be fully present for this. Let me take a short break first."
- "I'd like to take a moment to gather my thoughts before we proceed."
- "Can we reconvene in 15 minutes? I'd like to reset."

## Quick One-Liners

- "Stepping away for a reset."
- "Need a quick breather."
- "Taking five to recenter."
- "Going to reset - back shortly."
