# Words and Phrases That Cause Rupture

Phrases to be aware of and avoid - they harm communication and relationships.

- "Uh"
- "Duh"
- "Whatever"
- "Fine"
- "Jesus Christ"
- "Here we go again"
- "You always..." / "You never..."
- "I told you so"
- "Calm down"
- "Forget it"
- "I don't care"
- "Just drop it"
